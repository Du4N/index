Problem Statement:

Kurikuri Company, a pet food company, produces nutrient-rich dog food using chicken meal and fish meal as key ingredients.

Each unit of chicken meal costs P90.00 and contains 35 grams of protein, 25 grams of fat, and 1700 calories.

Each unit of fish meal costs P120.00 and contains 50 grams of protein, 40 grams of fat, and 1500 calories.

The company wants each batch of the final dog food product to contain at least 400 grams of protein, at least 300 grams of fat, and at least 10,000 calories.

How many units of chicken meal and fish meal should the company use to minimize costs while meeting these nutritional requirements?
