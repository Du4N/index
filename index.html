Kurikuri Pet Food Optimization

Problem Statement

Kurikuri Company, a pet food manufacturer, produces nutrient-rich dog food using chicken meal and fish meal as primary ingredients.

  • Each unit of chicken meal costs P90.00 and contains:
    • 35g protein
    • 25g fat
    • 1700 calories
  • Each unit of fish meal costs P120.00 and contains:
    • 50g protein
    • 40g fat
    • 1500 calories

The company needs each batch to contain at least:

  • 400g of protein
  • 300g of fat
  • 10,000 calories

Determine the optimal mix of chicken and fish meal that minimizes cost while meeting these nutritional requirements.
